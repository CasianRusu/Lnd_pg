Aerospace and Defense Trade Mission to Romania

Black Sea Strategies in collaboration with 47G, a leading US aerospace, defense, manufacturing, and finance ecosystem based in Utah, supported by the US Embassy Commercial Service in Romania, will host an Aerospace and Defense Event in Romania in March 2025.

This initiative is aimed at introducing U.S. companies to trade and investment opportunities in Romania's aerospace, defense, energy, and mining sectors, with a focus on expanding their outreach to other NATO & The EU member countries. This Trade Mission will focus on Romania and the Black Sea Countries as alternative regions for US companies interested in opening new markets as well as diversifying their supply chains operations - thus making an important contribution to political and military stability and security in the region.

The event aims to:

  • Introduce U.S. companies to trade and investment opportunities in Romania's aerospace, defense, manufacturing, and energy sectors, with potential expansion into other NATO and EU markets.

  • Facilitate Romanian companies access to the U.S. market by building strong business ties with 47G, one of the fastest- growing aerospace and defense ecosystems in the United States.

Why participate

Expansion Potential

47G, strategically based in Utah, helps Romanian companies enter the US market by providing access to a strong business network, trade, and financing opportunities. Conversely, as a Black Sea Strategies partner, 47G supports US businesses expanding into Romania, now part of Schengen. With its strategic location, growing economy, and major infrastructure and defense investments, Romania serves as a key hub for trade and investment in the EU, Black Sea region, and Central Asia.

Diversifying Supply Chains

By using Romania as a base for business expansion, US companies can diversify their supply chains, which is vital for building resilience against global supply chain interruptions and maintaining a consistent supply of goods and services.

Political and Military Stability

The involvement of US companies in Romania and the broader Black Sea region supports political and military stability. This engagement strengthens alliances, enhances security, and creates a stable environment conducive to long-term business activities.
